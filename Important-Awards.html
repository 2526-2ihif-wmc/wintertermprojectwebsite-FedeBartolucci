FIFA World Cup

First Awarded: 1930
Frequency: Every 4 Years
Most Wins: Brazil (5)

The most prestigious trophy in football, awarded to the winner of the FIFA World Cup tournament. It represents the pinnacle of international football achievement.

Recent Winners

  • ⭐ 2022: Argentina
  • ⭐ 2018: France
  • ⭐ 2014: Germany
⭐

UEFA Champions League

First Awarded: 1956
Frequency: Annual
Most Wins: Real Madrid (15)

Europe's elite club competition, bringing together the best teams from across the continent. Considered the most prestigious club trophy in world football.

Recent Winners

  • ⭐ 2024: Real Madrid
  • ⭐ 2023: Manchester City
  • ⭐ 2022: Real Madrid
🎖️

Ballon d'Or

First Awarded: 1956
Frequency: Annual
Most Wins: Lionel Messi (8)

The most prestigious individual award in football, given annually to the best male player in the world as voted by football journalists.

Recent Winners

  • ⭐ 2025: Ousmane Dembele
  • ⭐ 2024: Rodri
  • ⭐ 2023: Lionel Messi
🌎

Copa América

First Awarded: 1916
Frequency: Every 4 Years
Most Wins: Argentina (16)

The oldest international continental football competition, featuring South American national teams competing for regional supremacy.

Recent Winners

  • ⭐ 2024: Argentina
  • ⭐ 2021: Argentina
  • ⭐ 2019: Brazil
🇪🇺

UEFA European Championship

First Awarded: 1960
Frequency: Every 4 Years
Most Wins: Spain & Germany (3)

Europe's premier national team competition, often featuring football of the highest quality due to the continent's competitive strength.

Recent Winners

  • ⭐ 2024: Spain
  • ⭐ 2020: Italy
  • ⭐ 2016: Portugal
🎖️

Premier League Trophy

First Awarded: 1993
Frequency: Annual
Most Wins: Man United (13)

Awarded to the champion of England's top football league, considered one of the most competitive and watched domestic leagues in the world.

Recent Winners

  • ⭐ 2024: Manchester City
  • ⭐ 2023: Manchester City
  • ⭐ 2022: Manchester City
