Best footballers in football history

Football has produced unforgettable legends. These players are widely regarded as the greatest in history for their skill, achievements, and global impact.

Footballers and Infos

1. Lionel Messi • Forward • Argentina

Lionel Messi is widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time. Born in Rosario, Argentina, he became a club legend at FC Barcelona and led Argentina to the 2022 World Cup.

  • Goals: 800+
  • Ballon d'Or: 8
  • Major titles: La Liga ×10, UCL ×4
2. Cristiano Ronaldo • Forward • Portugal

Cristiano Ronaldo is known for athleticism, scoring and longevity. He starred for Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus and captained Portugal to Euro 2016 victory.

  • Goals: 900+
  • Ballon d'Or: 5
  • Major titles: UCL ×5, Euro 2016
3. Diego Maradona • Attacking Midfielder • Argentina

Diego Maradona is famous for creativity, dribbling and the legendary 1986 World Cup performance, including the "Goal of the Century".

  • Goals: 300+
  • World Cups: 1
  • Known for: 1986 World Cup
4. Pelé • Forward • Brazil

Pelé won three World Cups with Brazil and scored over 1000 goals. He is celebrated worldwide for his instinctive finishing and skill.

  • Goals: 1000+
  • World Cups: 3
  • Major: Santos & Brazil legend
